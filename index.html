How It Works

Three steps to make the web a better, safer place.

1. Spot a Bug

Find any publicly visible issue — a broken page, a typo, a slow-loading asset, a trademark violation, or anything else that needs fixing.

2. Submit a Report

Fill in the report form — or use the GitHub issue template directly. Tick the anonymous checkbox to submit privately via BLT-API.

3. Earn Recognition

Every accepted report adds to your leaderboard score. Commercial plan holders can also earn a share of subscription revenue from their reports.
